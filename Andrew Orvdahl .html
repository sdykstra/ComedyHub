Comedy Hub - Andrew Orvdahl

  • Andrew Orvedahl is a comedian and person currently based in Denver, Colorado.
    If you were a comedy sommelier, you might say his comedy is somewhat cerebral,
    with a dry finish and floral notes of sarcasm. He prefers mixing real-life stories
    with creative (and often improvised) observations within those stories. He is a
    regular at his home club, the renowned Comedy Works in Denver, and has performed
    at the Aspen, Las Vegas, Seattle, Great American, Bridgetown, and Montreal Just
    for Laughs comedy festivals.
